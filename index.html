Our mission our passion

Bedrock Real Food Kitchen is based within DW Fitness Club in Poole Dorset. Serving the members with awesome coffee and tailored unique selections of healthy food options.

Since we launched in January this year, we have brought a new lease of life to the club and it’s members are loving the taste of fresh roasted bean coffee, using the latest espresso machines and grinders to provide that perfect blend.

We also serve a large selection of teas, oven baked jacket potatoes, fresh salads, smoothies, and a great selection of supplements and snacks.

Pop in and see us soon!

p.s. Unfortunately we are currently only open to the members of the club, although they would prefer to keep a great cafe to themselves we are looking a ways to open to the public later this year. Watch this space!
